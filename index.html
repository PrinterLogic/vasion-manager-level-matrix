Building Talent

Networking & Recruiting

  • Ability to bring high-quality candidates to company from their personal network
  • Participation in team recruiting efforts (fairs, events, interviews)
  • Quality & strategy of recruiting
  • Occasionally refers high-quality candidates for hire.
  • Actively builds their external network.
  • Proactively participates in recruiting efforts, unprompted.
  • Has awareness of inequities, bias and the benefit of diverse teams; uses awareness to directly improve own recruiting efforts.
  • Writes job descriptions to fit organizational goals & culture as well as specific team needs.
  • Sometimes leads recruiting efforts & external representation of the team.
  • Takes primary responsibility for recruiting within their organization.
  • Maintains & grows a strong external network from which top talent can be pulled for strategic roles.
  • Consistently leads, directs and innovates in recruiting efforts for the larger organization.
  • Has strong awareness of inequities, bias and the benefit of diverse teams; uses awareness to drive accountability into their organization and achieve stronger teams.
  • Ensures execution of organizational recruiting vision within their organization.

  • Maintains & articulates a strong recruiting vision for the entire organization.
  • Effectively achieves overall organizational recruiting goals through use of external network and internal leaders.
  • Enforces accountability & reduction of bias in the recruiting process through clear directives and constant support of HR, Recruiting & Engineering teams.

Interviewing & Evaluation

  • Effective interviewing skills
  • Consistent and calibrated approach to candidate evaluation 
  • Involvement in final hiring decisions
  • Separates good vs. bad strategic hires based on experience & expected effects on their team, regardless of candidate seniority.
  • Actively works with peers & other leaders to calibrate interviewing & evaluation tactics.
  • Trains, monitors & improves own team’s interviewing skills & practices.
  • Holds all of their teams’ leaders and teammates accountable for excellent interviewing practices & results.
  • May be involved with individual contributor interviews for team accountability or company-selling purposes.
  • Effectively delegates details of technical & cultural interviewing to their managers.
  • Holds their organization accountable and takes primary responsibility for interviewing & evaluation mistakes or missteps.

  • Partners with HR, Recruiting and Engineering leaders to ensure best practices in interviewing & evaluation.

Onboarding

  • Involvement in the success of onboarding new hires
  • Team preparedness for and improvement of onboarding processes
  • Takes complete ownership for IC teammate onboarding success.
  • Solicits feedback & improves onboarding process for future teammates.
  • Ensures accountability for good onboarding experiences within their organization.


  • Connects the overall team vision to the specifics of onboarding and ensures accountability within the organization.

Teammate Growth & Retention

  • Responsibility for growing and retaining own teammates
  • Communicating & adjusting career paths
  • Ensuring sustainable workloads
  • Creating team succession plans
  • Takes primary responsibility for retaining teammates first within their team, and second within the organization.
  • Has a current career path & development plan in place for every direct report.
  • Closely monitors the workload and expectations placed on their team to maintain a healthy & desirable atmosphere.
  • Maintains a solid understanding of knowledge distribution and succession planning for their team to avoid SPOFs & gaps.
  • Ensures accountability for clearly communicated career paths, development plans, knowledge distribution reports and succession plans within their teams.
  • Plans with retention & attrition in mind to meet the strategic goals of their organization.
  • Constantly aware of teammates at risk of leaving within their organization, and works actively with leaders to find solutions.

  • Clearly articulates the value of teammate growth & retention with all Engineering leaders.
  • Ensures accountability for teammate growth & retention within the organization.
  • Performs diving saves with individual teammates when necessary or prudent.

Vision

Business Strategy

  • Understand and improve business strategy
  • Ensuring their decisions align with business strategy
  • Often contributes to business strategy based on their team experience.
  • Consistently contributes to business strategy.
  • Ensures accountability of their teams to align to overall business strategy.
  • Helps create and evolve the business strategy with cross-functional leaders.

  • Directly responsible for complete understanding and execution of the business strategy.
  • Works directly (or via delegates) with cross-functional leaders on the creation & refinement of business strategy.

Team Strategy Mapping

  • Create & articulate a team technical vision
  • Ensuring team technical vision aligns with product & business
  • Directly responsible for their teamU+2019s technical vision.
  • Consistently assures alignment of team strategy to evolving product & business strategy.
  • Ensures accountability of their teamsU+2019 creation & maintenance of technical visions, as well as their alignment with evolving product & business strategy.
  • Takes responsibility for cross-team alignment of evolving visions & strategies.

  • Connects organizational vision to specific technical visions.
  • Works with technical & organizational leaders to plan future vision & strategy for the organization.

Organizational Health

  • Using cross-team community building to understand and influence team morale
  • Mindful of organizational efficiency and works to improve it
  • Takes primary responsibility for the health, morale & efficiency of their team.
  • Calibrates & collaborates with peers & other leaders to improve cross-team health.
  • Ensures accountability of collaboration, calibration and success for the health, morale & efficiency of their organization.



  • Takes full responsibility for the health & efficiency of the organization.
